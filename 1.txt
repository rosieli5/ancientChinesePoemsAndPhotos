明月松间照，清泉石上流。
Translation: The bright moon shines through the pines, and the translucent spring flows through the rocks. 
Context: Two famous lines in the poem Shan Ju Qiu Ming (山居秋暝) by Wang Wei, created after or around 740 AD when the poet chose to retired into the peaceful mountain from the central bureaucratic system. Both the photo and the video are taken at People's State Forest, CT, 2020.
